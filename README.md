# M607 Computer Science Application Lab
Classwork and Practice

## Description  
Web development plays a pivotal role in shaping the digital landscape and is of paramount importance in todayâ€™s interconnected world. It serves as the backbone for the vast expanse of the internet, enabling individuals, businesses, and organizations to establish a robust online presence. In this module, students will learn advanced backend and frontend web development. Furthermore, students will learn to develop web applications in practice.

## Objectives  
Specific objectives include:  
1. To explore advanced concepts of web development.
2.  To build web applications in action.

## Learning Outcomes  
On successful completion of the module, students will be able to:  

1. Evaluate the business contexts that can benefit from web applications.
2. Analyze advanced web development concepts, including front-end and back-end development.
3. Build web applications in action.

## Teaching Methods  
Students will engage in learning through a series of directed study activities, lectures, and self-study. The teaching sessions will be divided into two parts. The first part belongs to an interactive lecture, where students learn about theoretical aspects of the presented topic. The second part will be an interactive lab, where students will implement the presented concepts themselves under the guidance of the tutor. Students will be also directed to online materials for further study. Note that teaching sessions will not be recorded by default, but the tutor may decide to record certain parts of lectures from time to time.
